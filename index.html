Who We Are

Competitive Programmers of KUET

SGIPC is a student-led club focused on algorithmic thinking, data structures, and regular contest participation. From first-year beginners to advanced coders, everyone gets a place to practice, ask, and improve together.

What We Do

Weekly rhythm that keeps everyone improving

Guided Problem Solving

Topic-wise problem lists, editorial discussions, and mentor hours for beginners, including guidance from mentors who were ICPC World Finalists.

Mock Contests

Timed contests based on real ICPC patterns, with post-contest analysis sessions before every ICPC and IUPC.

Team Preparation

Structured prep for regional contests with strategy drills and role-based team practice. We also arrange solo contests, and based on individual performance, we form teams for ICPC and IUPC.

Topic-Wise Expert Sessions

We arrange topic-wise sessions where experts in that topic lead the class and guide members through focused concepts and problems.
